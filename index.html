Coink

Coink is a money-saving tool that strives to help users plan and execute large purchases “goals”. Like goals, everyone’s financial situation is unique. Coink helps measure and monitor the user’s specific finances - income and expenditures - and provides insights to help make goals become reality

Infinity Closet

Infinity closet strives to break down all the barriers we currently seen in the clothing rental services market and make it more inclusive . It opens the door to male-gendered clothing rental services options, gender neutral options, as well as options for accessory rentals.
