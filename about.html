About Me

My Story

I am a UI/UX Designer currently based in New York City. I graduated from Tulane University with a bachelor's degree in communications. As a child, my teachers always described me as “curious” and my curiosity and excitement to learn in life has never stopped. The arts have always interested me as well as the world of digital technology. I took a coding course learning about HTML + CSS coding in my early college days and always had a hunger to learn more ever since. After graduation, I entered the recruitment field. The high energy and never ending process in the field excited me. With a background in recruitment, I offer a lot of experience in listening to the needs of my clients and candidates, executing a search and delivering the desirable talent, identifying pain points and presenting solutions that satisfy both the candidate and the client. Using my strong skills of listening and understanding when communicating needs and requirements. After a few years working in recruitment, always admiring the UI / UX design field from afar, I decided it was time to take a leap and change career paths into the area that has combined two of my interests. I’m ready to make a difference in the UI / UX world, helping people transform ideas into beautiful designs and products that are delightful to use and visually appealing.
